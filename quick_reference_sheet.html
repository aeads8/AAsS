Andrew's Asynchronous System (AAsS)

Core Mechanics Guide

1. The Roll Check

The core mechanic is based on a 3d6 roll, aiming for higher results. The formula is: 3d6 + Total Modifier.

Total Modifier Calculation:

Attribute Bonus + Temporary Bonus + Harm Penalty

Total Modifier

Thresholds (Target Number)

  • Critical Success: 15+
  • Full Success: 11-14
  • Mixed Success: 7-10
  • Critical Failure: 6-

2. Momentum & Tension

These two resources track the narrative flow and difficulty of the game. Momentum is the player resource, and Tension is the Game Master (GM) resource.

Momentum (Player)

Momentum is a reward for players given for successful checks. Momentum may be spent by players.

  • Gained on Full Success (+1) and Critical Success (+2).
  • Spent by the players to assist on rolls or with the scene.

Add Bonus

Cost: 1

Add +2 to any Skill Check before rolling.

Re-Roll

Cost: 2

Re-roll any result. You must accept the new result.

Declare Fact

Cost: 3

Define a minor, helpful fact about the scene.

Tension (GM)

Tension is a resource given to the GM when the player fails checks. Tension may be spent by the GM.

  • Gained on Mixed Success (+1) and Critical Failure (+2).
  • Spent by the GM to introduce complications, trigger enemy actions, or reinforce threats.

Complication

Cost: 1

Introduce a minor enemy, small hazard, or degrade a Full Success to Mixed.

Escalate

Cost: 2

Force an immediate, unexpected Skill Check or remove 1 Player Momentum.

Shift Scene

Cost: 3

Radically shift the environment to the PCs' disadvantage.

3. Harm Status (Damage Points)

Damage Points (DP) determine your current condition and the penalty applied to all Roll Checks.

Damage Points (DP) Status Effect / Penalty
0 Healthy No physical limitations. Penalty: 0
1–2 Bruised Minor wounds, discomfort. Penalty: -1
3–4 Injured Significant pain. Can only take minor actions. Penalty: -2
5+ Broken Incapacitated. Out of the fight. Penalty: Cannot make checks at all

4. Roll Outcomes Reference

Result Total Roll Effect
Critical Success 15+ Spectacular success. Gain 2 points of Momentum.
Full Success 11-14 Clean success. Gain 1 point of Momentum.
Mixed Success 7-10 Success with a cost. GM gains 1 point of Tension.
Critical Failure 6- Disastrous failure. GM gains 2 points of Tension.
